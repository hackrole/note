vim变量相关

Published: 五 08 一月 2016
By hackrole

In misc.

tags: vim vimscript

概述

vim里的变量通过let定义.

变量类型:

  1. 基本变量. let a = 'hello'
  2. 选项 let &number = 1. let &l:number=1
  3. 寄存器 let @a='hello'

变量的作用域:

  1. script. let s:a = 'hello'
  2. global. let g:a = 'hello'
  3. buffer. let b:a = 'hello'
